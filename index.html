About Me

Hi, I’m Catarina Jin, a student at the University of Waterloo studying Global Business and Digital Arts. My passions include creative design, storytelling, and digital media. I love working on projects that allow me to explore branding, illustration, and website design.

When I’m not studying or working on projects, I enjoy listening to music, traveling, and exploring new technologies. With a multicultural background and fluency in multiple languages, I bring a unique perspective to all my creative endeavors.
